About Me

Personal Background:

I like to think of myself as a dog and fitness enthusiast. I started CrossFit 5 years ago after many years doing traditional strength training. I started coaching because I wanted to share my knowledge and love for the sport with others. You can find my dog, Paisley, and I at our local CrossFit gym most mornings of the week.
I got into nutrition in high school after attending a horticultural camp in Hawaii. Ever since then I have been recreating all of my favorte, not so healthy dishes, into nutrious but still delious meals. For example, I made a purple sweet potato crust pizza. Before I went to culinary school, My instagram page was dedicated to healthy recipies. Since graduating, it has transformed into a page filled with dessert.
I have always been interested in cooking and baking because of my grandfather who was a professional chef. He cooked for the queen of england and entered competitions where he would create sculptures out of lard. His work and sucess has always been an inspiration to me!
Before deciding to go to culinary school, I spent a short time studying psychology, taking classes at William James and starting a masters program in clinical mental health counseling at Lesley University. Although, being a liscenced mental health professsional was not for me, I still use the knowledge i've gained in my everyday life.
My favorite things to do include CrossFit, cooking, baking, skiing, and traveling around the world.
